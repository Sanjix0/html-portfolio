The Best Movies According to BADR

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Harry Potter Series (2001-2011)

The magical journey of Harry Potter and his friends at Hogwarts as they battle the dark wizard Voldemort.

Spider-Man Series (2002-Present)

Follows the adventures of Peter Parker as he balances his life as a teenager and a superhero fighting villains like Green Goblin and Doctor Octopus

Supernatural Series (2005-2020)

Follows brothers Sam and Dean Winchester as they hunt demons, ghosts, and other supernatural creatures while exploring family, destiny, and the battle between good and evil.
